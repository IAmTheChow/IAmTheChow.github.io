Poor Education

In modern society education has become more and more important and it has also become a much broader topic. From primary education to tertiary and even computer education it is very important for almost everyone to have somekind of access to it. Currently in the world there are too many people that do not have access to even the most basic education, and even in first world countries it can be hard to even afford some levels of education.

Ghana, one of the countries that has the lowest literacy rates, had a 10% increase in literacy in just over 2 years although it may not seem like it education is slowly getting better and it has become a matter of time.
