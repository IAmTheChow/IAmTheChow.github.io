About Us:

This website was made to show information on all kinds of different problems when it comes to education around the world. This includes education of all levels including University and other tertiary levels as shown in the picture to the left. There is a huge problem in the world currently when it comes to education and it is not just effecting poorer countries, even in countries like America the costs of education is urealistic and many students find themselves stuck in student debt decades after university. This website also covers the issue of education when it comes to computers. Computers are becoming more and more essential in more and more sectors, too many people are poorly educated when it comes to computers and will begin to find themselves struggling to catch up in the ever changing world.
In the end this website will cover many topics when it comes to education and will sum up points that have been made online by many other websites, these websites will be linked below.
